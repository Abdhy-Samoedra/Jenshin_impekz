Mondstadt

The name Mondstadt itself comes from German which means City of the Moon. This region is one of the seven countries in Teyvat as well as the first place where Travelers look for their missing relatives. This country worships Barbatos, The Anemo Archon.

Liyue

Liyue is a port city surrounded by mountains and hills, which has a Chinese feel and is in the East of Tevyat. The Liyue region is divided into four, namely Mount Tianheng, Dihua Marsh, and Wangshu In and is equipped with small areas that must be visited.

Sumeru

Sumeru has deserts and forests. In the preview of the Bab Teyvat, Deinsleif describes Sumeru as a country filled with rainforests and deserts. Sumeru is an area that is said to have been inspired by Middle Eastern and South Asian cultures .
