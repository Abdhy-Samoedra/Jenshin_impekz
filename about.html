Game Feature

Game features in the Jenshin world that you can enjoy and play

Fantasy Adventure

Enjoy a riveting single-player adventure. As a traveler who came from another world, you will start your journey to find your brother who has been separated for a long time. You will also unravel the deepest mysteries of Teyvat, as well as yourself.

Explore Your World

Fly across a vast world, swim in crystal clear oceans, and climb towering mountains. Travel as much as you want and discover all the hidden secrets behind this world full of mystery and wonder.

Adventure Alone or Fight Together

You can fight solo, or you can invite your friends to fight together against dangerous monsters and explore the mysterious world of Jenshin in multi-platform on: PS4, iOS, Android and PC.
